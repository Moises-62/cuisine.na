Strawberry Shortcake Shake

Overview

This milkshake captures the essence of strawberry shortcake in a thick, sippable form. It combines vanilla ice cream with fresh or frozen strawberries, a touch of milk, and crumbled shortbread cookies. The result is a rich, creamy, and sweet dessert drink.

Preparation Details

Ingredients:

  • Vanilla Ice Cream (3 scoops)
  • Fresh or Frozen Strawberries (1 cup)
  • Milk (1/4 cup)
  • Shortbread Cookies (3-4 pieces, crumbled)
  • Vanilla Extract (1/2 tsp)
  • Whipped Cream (optional)
  • Strawberry Sauce (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Glass (Optional) Drizzle the inside of a tall milkshake glass with strawberry syrup or sauce for a decorative swirl. Place the glass in the freezer to chill while you prepare the shake.
  2. Combine Ingredients In a high-powered blender, add the milk, vanilla extract, vanilla ice cream, and strawberries.
  3. Add the Cake Crumble Crush the shortbread cookies into coarse crumbs. Add half of the crumbs to the blender (reserving the rest for garnish).
  4. Blend the Shake Secure the lid and blend the ingredients until smooth and creamy. If the shake is too thick, add milk 1 tablespoon at a time until you reach the desired consistency.
  5. Pour and Garnish Pour the shake into the chilled glass. Top generously with whipped cream, a drizzle of strawberry sauce, and the remaining shortbread cookie crumbs.
  6. Serve Immediately Serve the Strawberry Shortcake Milkshake immediately with a straw and a spoon.
