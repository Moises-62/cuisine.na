Pretzel

Overview

Soft Pretzels are made from a basic yeast dough that is briefly boiled in a baking soda solution before being baked. This process gives them their unique chewy texture, dark crust, and salty topping. They are perfect served warm with mustard or a cheese dipping sauce.

Preparation Details

Ingredients:

  • All-Purpose Flour (3 1/2 cups)
  • Warm Water (1 1/2 cups)
  • Active Dry Yeast (2 1/4 tsp)
  • Brown Sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 tsp, plus coarse salt for topping)
  • Unsalted Butter (2 tbsp, melted)
  • Water (8 cups, for bath)
  • Baking Soda (1/2 cup, for bath)
  • Egg Yolk (1 large, optional egg wash)

Steps:

  1. Activate the Yeast In a large bowl or stand mixer, combine the warm water, brown sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Mix the Dough Add the melted butter, salt, and flour to the yeast mixture. Mix on low speed (or by hand) until a shaggy dough forms. Increase speed and knead for 5-7 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  3. First Proof Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, turn to coat, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Shape the Pretzels Gently punch down the dough. Divide it into 8 equal pieces. Roll each piece into a long rope (about 24 inches long). Form each rope into the classic pretzel shape by crossing the ends, twisting them once, and pressing the ends onto the bottom curve of the rope.
  5. Prepare the Bath Preheat your oven to 450°F (232°C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper. In a wide pot, bring 8 cups of water and the 1/2 cup of baking soda to a rolling boil.
  6. Boil the Pretzels Using a slotted spatula, carefully dip each pretzel into the boiling baking soda bath for only 20 to 30 seconds. Do not over-boil. Remove the pretzel and let the excess water drain off.
  7. Bake and Finish Place the pretzels onto the prepared baking sheets. Brush the tops with the egg wash (if using) and sprinkle generously with coarse salt.
  8. Bake Place the baking sheets in the preheated oven and bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the pretzels are deep golden brown. Serve warm.
