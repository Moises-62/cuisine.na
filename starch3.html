Chickpea Salad

Overview

This recipe is a vegan and vegetarian take on traditional mayonnaise-based salads (like tuna or egg salad). Cooked chickpeas are roughly mashed and mixed with crunchy celery, onion, and a tangy, creamy dressing. It's excellent served cold on sandwiches, in lettuce cups, or with crackers.

Preparation Details

Ingredients:

  • Canned Chickpeas (2 cans, rinsed and drained)
  • Mayonnaise (1/4 cup)
  • Mustard (1 tbsp)
  • Celery (1/2 cup, diced)
  • Red Onion (1/4 cup, chopped)
  • Dill Pickles or Relish (2 tbsp)
  • Lemon Juice (1 tbsp, fresh)
  • Fresh Dill or Parsley (1 tbsp, optional)
  • Salt and Black Pepper (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Chickpeas Pour the rinsed and drained chickpeas into a medium bowl.
  2. Smash the Chickpeas Using a potato masher, fork, or the back of a large spoon, mash the chickpeas until they are mostly broken down but still retain some texture and chunkiness. Do not mash completely into a smooth paste.
  3. Mix the Base Add the mayonnaise, mustard, and fresh lemon juice to the mashed chickpeas. Stir until the mixture is creamy and well combined.
  4. Add the Crunch Fold in the diced celery, chopped red onion, and chopped pickles or relish. Add the chopped fresh dill or parsley (if using).
  5. Season and Chill Season the salad generously with salt and black pepper to taste. Mix gently one last time.
  6. Serve Serve the chickpea salad immediately, or cover and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes for the best flavor and texture. Serve on toasted bread, lettuce wraps, or with crackers.
