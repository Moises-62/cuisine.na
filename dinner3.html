Carbonnara

Overview

Carbonara is a creamy, indulgent adaptation of the Italian classic. It uses all-purpose cream or evaporated milk to create a rich, white sauce, differentiating it from the egg-based original. It often features sautéed garlic, bacon or ham, and is topped with shredded cheese and sometimes a raw egg yolk upon serving for extra richness.

Preparation Details

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti (500g)
  • Butter (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (6 cloves, minced)
  • Bacon or Ham (200g, cubed)
  • Mushrooms (1 cup, sliced, optional)
  • All-Purpose Cream (2 large cans)
  • Evaporated Milk (1 can, optional)
  • Cheese (1 cup, shredded)
  • Black Pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Pasta Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package directions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside, reserving about 1/2 cup of the cooking water.
  2. Sauté the Meats In a large skillet or wok, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the bacon or ham and cook until crispy. Remove the cooked meat and set aside, keeping the rendered fat in the skillet.
  3. Prepare the Aromatics Add the minced garlic to the skillet and sauté in the rendered fat for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the sliced mushrooms (if using) and cook until tender.
  4. Make the Cream Sauce Pour in the all-purpose cream (and evaporated milk, if using). Bring the mixture to a low simmer, stirring frequently. Do not let it boil vigorously.
  5. Thicken and Season Stir in the shredded cheese until it is fully melted and the sauce is smooth. Season the sauce generously with black pepper and salt to taste.
  6. Combine the Pasta Add the cooked and drained spaghetti to the skillet with the cream sauce. Toss well until the pasta is fully coated. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water.
  7. Finish and Serve Stir most of the crispy bacon/ham pieces back into the pasta. Serve immediately on individual plates, topped with the remaining crispy bacon, a sprinkle of parsley, and a raw egg yolk in the center (optional).
