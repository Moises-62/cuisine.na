Mediterranean Tuna Pasta

Overview

Mediterranean Tuna Pasta is a quick, vibrant dish that combines pantry staples like canned tuna and dried pasta with fresh, bright flavors from ingredients like lemon, capers, sun-dried tomatoes, and olives. It can be served warm or at room temperature, making it excellent for a weeknight dinner or a light lunch.

Preparation Details

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (350g, rotini or penne)
  • Canned Tuna (2 cans, packed in oil or water)
  • Sun-Dried Tomatoes (1/2 cup, sliced)
  • Black Olives (1/2 cup, pitted)
  • Capers (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Lemon (1 large, juiced and zested)
  • Fresh Parsley (1/2 cup, chopped)
  • Red Pepper Flakes (1/2 tsp, optional)
  • Olive Oil (1/4 cup)
  • Salt and Black Pepper (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Pasta Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente. Before draining, reserve about 1/2 cup of the starchy cooking water. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil (or the sun-dried tomato oil) in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using) and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant. Do not burn the garlic.
  3. Add the Mediterranean Flavors Stir in the sliced sun-dried tomatoes, black olives, and capers. Cook for 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Add Tuna and Lemon Reduce the heat to low. Add the drained tuna (flaked lightly) to the skillet. Stir in the fresh lemon juice and lemon zest.
  5. Moisten the Sauce Add the reserved pasta water to the skillet. Let the sauce simmer gently for 2 minutes to slightly thicken.
  6. Combine and Garnish Add the cooked and drained pasta to the skillet. Toss everything well to coat the pasta completely in the sauce. Stir in the chopped fresh parsley.
  7. Final Seasoning Taste and adjust the seasoning, adding salt and black pepper as needed. Serve immediately, or chill to serve as a cold pasta salad.
