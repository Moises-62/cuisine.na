Pistachio Ice Cream

Overview

Pistachio Ice Cream is a rich, slow-churned dessert made from an egg custard base (crème anglaise) infused with finely ground pistachios, almond extract (to enhance the pistachio flavor), and a touch of coloring for the signature green look. The custard base ensures a smooth, creamy texture.

Preparation Details

Ingredients:

  • Shelled Pistachios (1 cup, plus 1/4 cup chopped for mix-in)
  • Whole Milk (1 cup)
  • Heavy Cream (2 cups)
  • Sugar (3/4 cup)
  • Egg Yolks (4 large)
  • Vanilla Extract (1 tsp)
  • Almond Extract (1/4 tsp)
  • Green Food Coloring (a few drops, optional)
  • Salt (pinch)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Pistachios Grind 1 cup of shelled pistachios finely in a food processor until they resemble a coarse meal. Set aside 1/4 cup of the ground pistachios.
  2. Heat the Dairy In a medium saucepan, combine the whole milk, heavy cream, and the remaining ground pistachios (3/4 cup). Bring the mixture to a low simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat, cover, and let the pistachio flavor steep for 30 minutes.
  3. Temper the Eggs In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar, egg yolks, and salt until the mixture is pale yellow.
  4. Make the Custard Reheat the pistachio-infused cream mixture. Slowly pour about 1 cup of the hot cream mixture into the egg yolks while whisking vigorously (this is tempering). Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan with the remaining cream.
  5. Thicken the Custard Heat the custard over low heat, stirring constantly with a rubber spatula until the mixture thickens slightly and coats the back of the spoon (reaching about 170°F or 77°C). Do not boil.
  6. Chill the Base Strain the custard mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl to remove the pistachio solids. Stir in the vanilla extract, almond extract, and a few drops of green food coloring (if using). Cover and chill completely in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.
  7. Churn the Ice Cream Pour the chilled base into your ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's directions (usually 20-30 minutes).
  8. Final Freeze Stir in the reserved 1/4 cup of chopped pistachios (if using). Transfer the soft ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 3-4 hours to harden before serving.
